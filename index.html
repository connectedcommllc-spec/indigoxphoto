Our process

What happens after you book.

The process is simple, professional, and built to keep your listing moving. Once you book, we confirm the appointment, schedule the shoot, capture the property, and deliver the final media fast.

1. Book your shoot

Choose the services you need and submit your booking request so we can lock in the appointment.

2. We confirm and schedule

Once the request is received, the appointment is confirmed and the shoot details are finalized for the property.

3. We photograph the home

On shoot day, we capture the property with the services selected, whether that includes photos, drone, video, reels, or twilight coverage.

4. Delivery within 24 hours

Final media is delivered quickly so the listing can go live with polished, professional content as soon as possible.

Appointment should be ready to shoot upon arrival

Please prep the client so the home is fully ready before the appointment begins. The property should be cleaned, decluttered, photo-ready, and accessible at arrival so the shoot can stay efficient and the final media looks its best.
