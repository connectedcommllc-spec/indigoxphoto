Social media reel advice

Reels should feel cinematic, clear, and strategically useful.

Luxury reel strategy should not feel cheap. Instead of over-cutting every clip, strong reels create curiosity while still giving the viewer enough detail to understand the mood, the layout, and why the property is special.

1. Hook the viewer with architecture or mood

Start with the strongest shot: a premium exterior, a dramatic hallway, a pool at twilight, or a cinematic kitchen move. The first second should instantly signal quality.

2. Show flow, not just random details

Luxury buyers and sellers respond to clarity. A reel should help the viewer feel how the rooms connect, not just flash five unrelated clips with no context.

3. Keep the edit premium

Let shots breathe. Use movement with intention. Strong reels are about atmosphere and emotional clarity, not only speed.

4. Give agents branded versions

Offer both property-led reels and agent-branded edits so the content supports the listing and the agent’s personal marketing ecosystem.
